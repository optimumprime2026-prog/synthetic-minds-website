# Homepage Wireframe — Synthetic Minds

## Goal of the homepage
The homepage should immediately communicate what Synthetic Minds does, why it matters now, and what action a visitor should take next. The main conversion goal is to get qualified visitors to book a call or request a consultation.

## Primary CTA
- Book a call

## Secondary CTA
- Request a consultation

## Homepage structure

### 1. Hero section
**Purpose:** Communicate positioning and value in one glance.

**Suggested content:**
- Headline: The Full-Stack Compute Efficiency Company
- Subheadline: We help organizations reduce AI infrastructure waste, optimize compute performance, and scale intelligent systems with confidence.
- CTA 1: Book a call
- CTA 2: Request a consultation
- Supporting visual: abstract compute / infrastructure / AI visual or clean product-style illustration

### 2. Problem section
**Purpose:** Make the pain obvious.

**Suggested content:**
- Rising compute costs
- Limited GPU availability
- AI becoming business-critical
- Lack of visibility into real infrastructure bottlenecks

**Format:** 3–4 pain cards with short copy

### 3. Solution section
**Purpose:** Show the full-stack approach clearly.

**Suggested content:**
Three solution pillars:
1. AI Agents as a Service
2. Compute Optimization Services
3. Optimized Enterprise Infrastructure

**Format:** 3-column solution block or staircase diagram

### 4. How it works section
**Purpose:** Show progression and build trust.

**Suggested flow:**
- Assess
- Optimize
- Scale

Explain how Synthetic Minds enters through practical use cases, then expands into optimization and infrastructure.

### 5. Industry focus section
**Purpose:** Show relevance and specialization.

**Industries:**
- Healthcare
- Pharmaceutical & Life Sciences
- Food & Agriculture
- Government & Universities

**Format:** industry cards with one-line explanation each

### 6. Why Synthetic Minds section
**Purpose:** Differentiate from generic AI agencies and resellers.

**Suggested points:**
- Combines software and hardware optimization
- NVIDIA-oriented infrastructure thinking
- Commercial and technical founder combination
- Vertical access and domain credibility

### 7. Founders / credibility section
**Purpose:** Show who is behind the company.

**Suggested content:**
- Pascal de Wit
- Elger Bouwman
- short founder positioning
- optionally logos, background references, or prior company mentions

### 8. CTA section
**Purpose:** Push action after credibility is built.

**Suggested content:**
- Headline: Ready to make AI infrastructure efficient?
- CTA: Book a call
- Secondary CTA: Request a consultation

### 9. Footer
**Suggested items:**
- navigation
- contact
- legal pages
- LinkedIn

## UX notes
- Keep homepage high-trust and enterprise-oriented
- Avoid hypey AI visuals or vague buzzwords
- Use clean sections, short copy, and strong hierarchy
- Make the CTA visible above the fold and repeated below
- Optimize for decision-makers, not general consumers

## Suggested page tone
- Clear
- Technical but accessible
- Confident
- Enterprise-focused
- Not flashy
